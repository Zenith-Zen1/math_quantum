## Goals
### Interested in studying cyptography
I am interested in studying how cryptography uses mathematical algorithms to secure information. This project would allow me to explore encryption systems such as RSA and understand the mathematical processes that make them secure.

### Interested in taking quantum computing classes
In the future, I hope to take quantum computing classes to world with *qubits, quantum gates, circuits, and algorithms* that I learned during this program. This project would give me an introduction to how quantum principles can be applied to problems in cybersecurity.

### Interested in working at the intersection of cryptography and QIS
Quantum computers could both challenge existing encryption systems and enable new forms of secure communication. I am interested in exploring this intersection through topics such as *Shor’s algorithm* and *post-quantum cryptography*.

### Interested in understanding how to mitigate risks associated with the introduction of QIS to cryptography
Powerful quantum computers will certainly threaten encryption methods currently used to protect information. I want to understand these risks and investigate possible solutions, including quantum-resistant algorithms and the transition to post-quantum cryptographic standards.
